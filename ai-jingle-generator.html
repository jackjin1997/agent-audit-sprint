Commercial-use guardrails

Position the offer around practical use, not vague ownership claims

Paid AI music tools can support commercial use, but copyright registration and platform policies still vary. This service should disclose the tool used, avoid imitating known artists, keep human-written brand lines separate, and deliver a usage memo with each paid package.
