## Safe Outreach Rules

- Do not claim copyright ownership, registration certainty, or legal clearance.
- Do not ask for private campaign data, customer data, unreleased scripts, or third-party lyrics.
- Do not imitate named artists or clone living voices.
- Do not post generic ads in communities.
- Use one concrete reason the recipient might need short branded audio.
- Send at most one follow-up.
- Count revenue only after payment is verified against an accepted jingle brief.

## Qualification Checklist

- The prospect already publishes audio/video or runs paid/social ads.
- They have a brand, show, slogan, event, sponsor, or recurring segment.
- A 15/30/60 second audio asset would be useful without a long strategy project.
- They can approve a simple brief.
- Contact route is public and appropriate for commercial inquiry.
- The message can be personalized in one sentence.
